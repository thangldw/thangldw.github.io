About

I'm a data-driven problem solver who works at the seam between engineering and the business. My favorite work starts in ambiguity — a messy dataset, a vague AI ambition, a customer who knows the pain but not the solution — and ends with something running in production that people rely on.

As a Forward Deployed Engineer, I don't hand off a spec and disappear. I sit with the team, learn their domain, and build alongside them: the pipelines, the models, the LLM systems, and the guardrails that make them safe to trust.

I care about leverage over output — the smallest system that solves the real problem, documented so the next engineer can own it. Minimalist by temperament, pragmatic by trade.

02

How I work

Embed & discover

I start where the problem lives — with the users and the data. Before writing code I reframe the ask until the real constraint is clear. Most AI projects fail on the problem, not the model.

Build & ship

Production-grade from day one: reliable pipelines, evaluated models, LLM systems with retrieval and guardrails, deployed on cloud infra that won't page you at 3am. Fast, but not fragile.

Scale & enable

The deliverable isn't code — it's a capable team. I document, mentor, and hand over systems people understand and can extend long after I'm gone.
