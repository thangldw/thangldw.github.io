About

I'm a data-driven problem solver who has spent the last decade in Tokyo working at the seam between engineering and the business — starting in statistical analysis, moving through data engineering, and now leading technical delivery as a Front Technical Lead at FPT Japan.

My favorite work starts in ambiguity: a messy dataset, a vague ambition, a customer who knows the pain but not the solution. I consult on the architecture, build the pipelines and models, and lead teams of 30–50 engineers (including offshore) to ship the full lifecycle — while handling the pre-sales, negotiation and stakeholder work that keeps everyone aligned.

I care about leverage over output — the smallest system that solves the real problem, documented so the next engineer can own it. Stay steadfast, stay free.
