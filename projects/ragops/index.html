The product idea

Teams can create impressive AI pilots quickly. The harder production question is: is this candidate safe and useful enough to release?

I designed RAGOps as the evidence layer between an AI change and that decision. The core evaluates portable scenarios and traces; the reference Japanese troubleshooting agent demonstrates ACL-first graph-assisted retrieval, approval-aware workflow decisions, and controlled rollout thinking.

The portfolio page stays intentionally concise. The standalone showcase contains the full discovery story, architecture, experiment, limitations, and rollout recommendation.
