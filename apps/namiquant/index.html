Operating loop

From observation to accountable follow-up

The workflow makes uncertainty visible and preserves the reasoning around a decision.

  1. 01 · ObserveNotice material change

    Capture the event without immediately converting it into an action.

  2. 02 · ResearchBuild the evidence set

    Frame questions, compare sources, and record what remains uncertain.

  3. 03 · Review riskChallenge the thesis

    Make constraints and failure conditions explicit before proceeding.

  4. 04 · DecideRecord human judgment

    Preserve the conclusion, its evidence window, and open reservations.

  5. 05 · MonitorReturn when facts change

    Schedule follow-up and evaluate whether the original reasoning still holds.
